About

Hi! My name is Theisen (pronounced "TY sen"). I am a senior in Computer Science at Iowa State University. I will be graduating from Iowa State in December 2013.

Although newer to the the software development world, I have made up for that with a drive to learn and experience new things. In just the last two years I have become quite accomplished at a select few programming languages, while making sure I have a little bit of knowledge in a lot of places. My experience comes mainly from my classes at Iowa State and my internship at WebFilings which I began in Nov. 2011.

It's hard to believe that in just 2 years I've gone from an Intro to Java course to a course in Artificial Intelligence. I've also taken several database systems, project management, and operating system classes on my adventure at Iowa State.

While attending ISU, I joined the WebFilings company in its early stages and it has been amazing to see it grow, and to grow along with it. When I joined, I had a half-semester of programming experience so I spent most of my time on manual regression testing. Quickly though, I was able to put what I was learning in school to use. I moved to a QA Test Dev position where I worked on a team that created an automated test builder to eliminate the need for our manual regression testing. Finally, I was able to move into a developer role where I could experience the pressure of customer-facing changes and full-on agile methodology.
